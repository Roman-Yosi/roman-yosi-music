Roman Yosi (Rohman) — Independent Music Artist from Indonesia

Press Article • Official Artist Profile

Introduction

Roman Yosi, also known as Rohman, is an independent music artist and digital creator from Indonesia. He originates from Lebak, Banten, and is actively involved in the modern digital music industry. His work represents the growing movement of independent musicians who build their careers through online platforms without major label support.

Background

From an early stage, Roman Yosi developed an interest in music creation and digital content production. He began exploring music as a form of personal expression and gradually developed a self-managed approach to production and distribution. Instead of relying on traditional music industry structures, he focuses on independent development and digital platforms.

Music Career

Roman Yosi builds his music career independently by releasing songs through platforms such as Spotify, YouTube, and TikTok. His workflow includes self-production, distribution management, and digital branding. This independent approach allows him to maintain full creative control over his artistic direction.

As an independent artist, he continues to expand his presence in the digital music ecosystem, focusing on consistent releases and long-term audience growth.

Musical Style

His musical style blends Indie Pop, Acoustic Pop, and Cinematic Music elements. The sound is often emotional, atmospheric, and story-driven, focusing on themes such as love, loss, hope, and personal reflection.

This combination creates a distinctive identity that aligns with modern independent music trends, where storytelling and emotional depth are key elements of artistic expression.
