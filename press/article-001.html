Roman Yosi (Rohman) — Independent Music Artist from Indonesia

Published: May 2026

About the Artist

Roman Yosi, also known as Rohman, is an independent music artist and digital creator from Indonesia. He originates from Lebak, Banten, and is active in the modern digital music industry.

He builds his career independently through digital platforms without major label support, focusing on self-production, digital distribution, and long-term artistic development.

Music Career

Roman Yosi develops and releases his music through platforms such as Spotify, YouTube, and TikTok. His approach emphasizes independent production and consistent release strategy in the digital music ecosystem.

As an independent artist, he manages his own creative direction, branding, and music distribution, allowing full control over his artistic identity and output.

Musical Style

His music style blends Indie Pop, Acoustic Pop, and Cinematic Music. The sound is often characterized by emotional depth, atmospheric production, and storytelling-driven composition.

His work explores themes such as personal experience, relationships, emotional reflection, life struggles, hope, and human connection.
